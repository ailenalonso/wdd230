Vicente Lopez History

Vicente López, a centennial and modern city at the same time, has an unusual diversity, which offers residents and visitors various alternatives and the possibility of living a unique experience: history, culture, sports, tourism, nature.

Nature

Vicente López is a city that breathes its green spaces, and the best way to enjoy our flora 4 and fauna is with some mates on our public coast. A route that goes from Hipólito Yrigoyen to General Paz and offers extensive green spaces, a walkway near the river, a bike path and different areas prepared for all kinds of sports.

Points of Interest – Ader Tower

Florentine-style monument from the 18th century, 42.30 meters high and 217 Carrara marble steps separate the main hall from the viewpoint.

Lumiton Museum of Film

Lumiton was the first film production company created in Argentina in 1931. The production company installed its studios in Munro, Vicente López's district, in the industrial belt of Greater Buenos Aires.

Committed to the Environment
